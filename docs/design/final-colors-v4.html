Color Usage Rules

Teal

Primary brand color. Use for "SPM" in logo, primary buttons, links, and key interactive elements.

Orange

Personal brand accent. Use for "Toddfather", pricing, service tiers, and warm emphasis points.

Purple = AI

Two purples for AI content. Primary (#8241C8) for badges/icons, Dark (#58108E) for backgrounds/shadows.

Lime

Success and governance. Use for positive outcomes, good scores, and governance-related content.

Hot Pink

Problems and alerts. Use for broken plans, disputes, errors, and problem agitation sections.

White/Gray

Text hierarchy. Primary (#E2E8F0) for headlines, muted (#94A3B8) for secondary content.
